How it works

Four steps, about a minute.

  1. 1
    Upload

    Drop in a PNG/JPG. Nothing is sent anywhere — it’s analysed locally.

    Read the grade

  3. 3

    Apply a real correction to your image and compare — or just absorb the lesson. You decide.

What ArtGrader is for

Think of this as a patient studio tutor that never gets tired of looking at your work. It won’t judge your taste — it reads the fundamentals (the stuff that’s actually measurable) and shows you, in plain language and real theory, where a piece is strong and where it’s leaking.

One honest note: a deliberately flat, desaturated, low-contrast style may “score” lower even when it’s a great intentional choice. Read the grade as “here’s what my pixels are objectively doing” — then decide for yourself what’s a choice and what’s a mistake. That judgment is the artist’s job. Always.
